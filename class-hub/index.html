Announcement
Welcome — we're your Class of 2028 representatives.
Hi everybody! Both Amelia and I feel incredibly honored and excited to have been elected as your class reps. We built this hub as a way to help keep you all informed, connected, and supported throughout the program. Our intention is to formally update you all weekly to biweekly with recaps of our meetings with faculty and leadership, share upcoming events, and log the status of anything you bring to us. This space is truly for you.
